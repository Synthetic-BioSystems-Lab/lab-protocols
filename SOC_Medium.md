# Steps
- 500 mL 1 M Glucose solution
1. Fill the bottle with 400 mL distilled water
2. Measure 90.08 g Glucose
3. Add distilled water to a totalof 500 mL
4. Filter stleriize with a 0.2 µm filter

- 250 mL 250 mM Potassium chloride
1. Fill the bottle with 200 mL distilled water
2. Measure 4.66 g Potassium chloride
3.Add distilled water to a totalof 250 mL
4. Autoclave solution at 121 °C for 00:15:00

- 125 mL 2 M Magnesium chloride
1. Fill the bottle with 100 mL distlled water
2. 10 Measure 50.825 g Potassium chloride
3. Add distilled water to a totalof 125 mL
4. Autoclave solution at 121 °C for 00:15:00

- 1000 mL S.O. B. medium
1. Fill the bottle with 950 mL distilled water
2. Measure 20 g Tryptone, 5 g Yeast extract and 0.5 g Sodium chloride. (Optional: Add 15 g Agar to create agar plates)
3. Add 1 mL Potassium chloride solution (250 millimolar (mM) ) and 0.5 mL Magnesium chloride solution (2 Molarity (M))
4. Adjust pH to 7.0 with concentrated sodium hydroxide
5. Add distilled water to a total of 1000 mL
6. Autoclave solution at 121 °C 

- 100 mL S.O.C. medium
1. Sterilely transfer 98 mL S.O.B. medium to a sterile 100 mL container
2. Sterilely add 2 mL 1 Molarity (M) Glucose solution
