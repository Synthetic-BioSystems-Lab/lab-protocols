# Ingredients:
- Duran Bottle 250ml
- Distilled Water
- LB broth with agar powder

# Steps:
1. Add 200ml of distilled water to 250ml Duran bottle
2. Look up LB agar manufacturer's instruction on concentration and calculated needed mass of powder
3. Suspend the calculated mass of LB broth powder in 200ml of the distilled water
4. Shake bottle until powder is completely dissolved with no clumps left
5. Untighten lid of Duran bottle until there is no resistance
6. Autoclave for 15 minutes at 121Â°C
