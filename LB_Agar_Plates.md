# Ingredients:
- Petri Dishes
- LB agar
- Antibiotic
- Bunsen burner

# Steps:
1. Label the bottom of the petri dish with the following information: date, initials, antibiotic, plasmid, stain
2. Reheat LB agar in microwave in 30 second intervals if necessary until solution is completely homogenous
3. Light bunsen burner and work close to it
4. Add 20 ul of antibiotic (1000x) to 20 ml of LB agar
5. Pour ~20 ml of ~55ÂºC LB agar into petri dish
6. Gently swirl the petri dish on the table to ensure bobbles are attached to the sides
7. Cover half of petri dish with its lid and leave around bunsen burner to evaporate condensation for 30 to 60 minutes
8. Stack petri dishes, tape around them, and store in the fridge
