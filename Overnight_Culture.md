# Ingredients:
- LB broth
- Selected antibiotic
- Culture tube
- Inoculation loop

# Steps:
1. Pour 10ml of LB broth into culture tube
2. Add 10 ul of selected antibiotic (1ul/ml)
3. Using an inoculation loop, carefully select your isolated colony from the media plate and shake the loop in media until the fragment has come loose and is visibly floating in media
4. Loosley close the culture tube
5. Place the tube in shacking incubator at ~140tpm at the optimal growing temperature of your bacteria (37C)
6. Leave the liquid culture to grow overnight for approximately 12-14 hrs
