# Steps
1. Measure out 0.4g of agarose
2. Mix agarose powder with 40 mL 1xTAE in a microwavable flask
3. Heat flask in 30 seconds intervals in the microwave swirling in between until agarose is dissolved
4. Let agarose solution cool down to about 50 Â°C (about when you can comfortably keep your hand on the flask), about 5 mins
5. Level the casting tray and add the TAE mix
6. Add 4ul of 10000x dye and mix
7. Add well comb
8. Let set for 1-2 hours
