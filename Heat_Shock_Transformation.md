# Ingredients:
- Thermocycler
- PCR tubes
- Eppendorf tubes
- LB broth
- Mini-prepped DNA plasmids
- Competent cells

# Steps:
1. Add 40 ul of competent cells to PCR tube
2. Add 5 ul of mini-prepped DNA
3. Put PCR tube in the thermocycler and run at 0C for 20 minutes
4. Run 42C for 1 minute
5. Run 0C for 3 minutes
6. Run 25C until samples are picked up
7. Add 1 ml of LB broth to an Eppendorf tube
8. Add full volume of transformed cells to the 1 ml of LB broth
9. Tape Eppendorf to the shaking shield and incubate for 1-2h
