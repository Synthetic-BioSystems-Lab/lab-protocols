# Ingredients:
- Duran Bottle (500ml)
- Distilled Water
- LB broth powder


# Steps:
1. Add 400ml of distilled water to 500ml Duran bottle
2. Look up LB agar manufacturer's instruction on concentration and calculated needed mass of powder
3. Suspend the calculated mass of LB broth powder in 400ml of the distilled water
4. Swirl Duran bottle until powder is completely dissolved with no clumps left
5. Autoclave for 1:30h minutes at 121 Â°C
